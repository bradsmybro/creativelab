Pokemon GO is a game that has experienced widespread popularity across the world! Below are some interesting facts about this almost three year old game.

Pokemon GO was so popular in the months after it was released that more than just their servers where overloaded. In Provo, Utah due to four pokemon stops being placed close together in the game the library lawn was packed full of players daily. Sadly as announced here so many people were playing that it became a strain on the library and they had to request that the Pokemon GO stops be removed.

As of May 2018 Pokemon go has been downloaded over 800 million times.

There are multiple enourmous gatherings of fans that take place across the world throughout the world. Called Safari Zones they are extremely popular with event appearing in America, Europe, Asia, and recently South America! Attendees get to experience increased rates of pokemon appearance as well as meeting thousands of other Pokemon GO players.
